About Us

The International Conference on Microelectronics Devices, Circuits and Systems (ICMDCS 2017) will be held at the VIT University, Vellore India from 10th to 12th August 2017. ICMDCS 2017 is organized by Department of Micro and Nanoelectronics and technically co-sponsored by Global Foundries . The conference covers the subject areas including: digital IC design, analog/RF/Mixed Signal IC design, Device Modeling and Technology, RF communication circuits, embedded systems; nonlinear circuits and system In addition to the technical papers, the conference also covers tutorials on recent advancements in the above said areas, Keynote and Plenary sessions by leading industry leaders and renowned academicians.​

VIT UNIVERSITY

VIT University was started in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College by Dr.G.Viswanathan, Founder & Chancellor. The institute was upgraded to a University conferred in recognition of its academic excellence in 2001 by the Ministry of Human Resources Development, Government of India. VIT today comprises of twelve constituent Schools and interdisciplinary Centers offering 18 undergraduate, 31 postgraduate and research programs upto PhD level and more than 1400 fulltime faculty members work at VIT. The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research spurs us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. VIT ranked No.1 private engineering institute by MHRD [NIRF-2016 ranking]. Call for papers

ICMDCS2017 solicits to submit the full length research papers (maximum 6 pages) with an abstract of maximum 200 words and up to six pages for technical content including figures, tables and references. Manuscripts that are being submitted to this conference should not be published or currently under review on any other conference, symposium, workshop or journal. Such manuscripts will not be considered for publication. Manuscript format should follow the standard IEEE conference template. Manuscripts can be submitted under the wide ranges of fields mentioned below but are not limited to these topics.

DIGITAL DESIGN

Logic and physical synthesis, physical design, timing and signal integrity, power integrity, design for Manufacturability, design for yield, design challenges for advanced technology nodes, low-power design, power-aware and energy-efficient design, thermal management, battery-aware design, energy harvesting, CAD Tools, Verification methodologies, DFT, fault modeling and simulation, ATPG, BIST, board-level and system-level test, memory test

ANALOG, MIXED-SIGNAL AND RF DESIGN

Design of analog, mixed-signal, and RF IP, high-speed wired and wireless communication interfaces, low-power analog and RF, memory design, standard cell design

Technology & Devices

Logic and Memory devices: Beyond CMOS devices, MOSFET-like structures with steep subthreshold slope, MOSFETs with high-mobility channel materials; Optimization of junctions and contacts; Fundamental understanding of non-volatile memory cells; Two-terminal selection devices for memory arrays; Revolutionary SRAM and DRAM replacement solutions; Simulation/modeling for processes, materials, phenomena, and devices Flexible and Organic Electronics: Materials and manufacturing processes for flexible electronics, Flexible transistors and ICs for logic computation, memory, displays, and analog and RF applications, Flexible sensors and actuators; reliability and thermal management of flexible electronics, Applications in displays, lighting, energy harvesting, storage, vehicular, robotics, wearable/implantable electronics. Photovoltaics Nano-electro-mechanical Systems and Sensors: Photovoltaics using Organic, Crystalline Silicon, Amorphous, Nanostructured, Silicon Film, and Thin Film. Characterization and control of PV systems, Nano/bio-sensing and robots, Nano materials for NEMS, Nano fluidics. Optoelectronics, Photonics and Compound Semiconductor Devices: Synthesis and characterization of carbon-based optical materials, 2-D materials beyond graphene, Linear and nonlinear optical spectroscopy of carbon-based and other 1-D and 2-D materials, Electronic and transport properties of ultrathin carbon films, Application of nanocarbon materials for lasing, Nanocarbon-based solar cells, Growth and related technologies, High-frequency and high-power devices, GaN and related semiconductors, Oxide semiconductors, Organic semiconductors and flexible materials​.

SYSTEM LEVEL DESIGN

System-level design methodologies, processor and memory design, multicore systems, GPU design, on-chip communication architectures and networks-on-chip, performance analysis, defect-tolerant architectures, machine learning), : Hardware/Software co-design, embedded SoC, embedded multi-core systems, board-level hardware, hardware platforms for Internet-of-Things (IoT) devices,Reconfigurable computing, FPGA architecture and FPGA circuit design, CAD for FPGA, FPGA prototyping, FPGA-based accelerators for cloud servers,Wireless sensor networks, low-power wireless systems, wireless protocols, wireless power delivery
