About Us

The International Conference on Microelectronics Devices, Circuits and Systems (ICMDCS 2017) will be held at the VIT University, Vellore India from 10th to 12th A ust 2017. ICMDCS 2017 is organized by Department of Micro and Nanoelectronics is technically sponsored by IEEE Electronic Devices Society and technically co-sponsored by Globalfoundries.

The conference covers the subject areas including: Digital IC design, Analog/RF/Mixed Signal IC design, Device Modeling and Technology, RF communication circuits, Communication Technologies, Signal, Image and Video Processing,  Embedded systems,  Nonlinear circuits and system.  In addition to the technical papers, the conference also covers tutorials on recent advancements in the above said areas, Keynote and Plenary sessions by leading industry leaders and renowned academicians.â€‹

VIT UNIVERSITY

VIT University was started in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College by Dr.G.Viswanathan, Founder & Chancellor. The institute was upgraded to a University conferred in recognition of its academic excellence in 2001 by the Ministry of Human Resources Development, Government of India. VIT today comprises of twelve constituent Schools and interdisciplinary Centers offering 18 undergraduate, 31 postgraduate and research programs upto PhD level and more than 1400 fulltime faculty members work at VIT. The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research spurs us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. VIT ranked No.1 private engineering institute by MHRD [NIRF-2016 ranking]. With steady steps, we continue our march forward. More...

Information For Authors

Call for papers

Authors must submit their papers using easychair (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icmdcs2017)  on or before 20th May 2017. All papers will be reviewed for technical content and scope by a technical program committee.

All accepted and presented papers would be submitted to digitally archived through IEEE Xplore and indexed in Scopus. (IEEE Xplore Catalog Number: CFP17L10-ART, ISBN: 978-1-5386-1716-8).

Standard IEEE conference templates for LaTeX and Microsoft Word formats can be obtained from:  http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html.  The page limits are SIX pages for regular papers and THREE pages for short papers. Submitted papers must be unpublished and not currently under review for any other publication. Authors of accepted papers must sign an IEEE copyright release form and also present their paper at the conference to be included in IEEE Xplore. If you have any questions regarding the submission of manuscripts, please contact the organizer through email: icmdcs2017@gmail.com

Publication Details

  1. All the accepted papers which are registered for ICMDCS2017 will be published in the conference proceedings with ISBN number (assigned by IEEE, USA) and also submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore.
  2. Selected and Extended version of Papers will be submitted into:
    • SCI-E Journal(Annexure-1)
    • Scopus Indexed Journals(Annexure-2)
    • Other reputed journals.
    • Book Chapters.
  3. IEEE Policies
    : IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g., removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference. Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission). These matters are taken very seriously, and the IEEE Communications Society will take action against any author who engages in either practice. Follow these links to learn more on plagiarism and double submission: IEEE Policy on Plagiarism and IEEE Policy on Double Submission.
